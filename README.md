## 📌 Overview

**IronCore Fitness2** transforms the gym membership experience into a luxurious digital journey. Users can browse membership plans (Basic to Elite), register with photo upload, select training programs, complete a demo payment, and instantly receive a flip‑animated, printable Gym ID Card. A dashboard, admin panel, and trainer login mockups round out the enterprise feel.

Perfect for academic projects, UI/UX portfolios, or as a starting point for a full‑stack gym management solution.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

- **Premium Dark UI** – Deep black (`#050505`) background with luxurious gold and red highlights.
- **Black & Gold Palette** – Primary red (`#E63946`) and secondary yellow (`#FFD60A`) for a powerful, energetic brand.
- **Glassmorphism** – Semi‑transparent cards that float above the background with soft blur.
- **3D Motion UI** – Tilt‑sensitive cards, flip‑animated ID card, and floating particle effects.
- **Luxury Gym Aesthetic** – Bold typography (Bebas Neue), neon glows, and smooth transitions that echo modern fitness brands.

## ✨ Key Features

- **10+ Interconnected Pages** – Home, Login (member/trainer/admin), Register, Membership Plans, Training Programs, Admission, Payment, Dashboard, Gym ID Card, Admin, About Developer, Contact.
- **Complete Membership Flow** – Browse plans → Select plan → Fill admission form (with photo upload) → Demo payment → Receive animated, printable ID Card.
- **Membership Plans** – Four tiers with visual selection: Basic (₹999), Standard (₹1999), Premium (₹2999), Elite (₹4999).
- **Training Programs** – 12 categories (Weight Training, Yoga, HIIT, etc.) displayed as interactive cards.
- **Admission Form** – Personal details, medical declaration, photo upload with live preview, plan selection, and training choice.
- **Auto‑calculated Pricing** – Plan price dynamically shown during payment.
- **Flip‑animated Gym ID Card** – Front: member photo, name, ID, QR placeholder. Back: emergency contact, expiry. Print/download via browser.
- **Admin / Trainer Login** – Placeholder dashboards with statistics.
- **BMI Calculator** – Instant BMI feedback from weight/height inputs.
- **Responsive Design** – Adapts to desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **Glassmorphism Navbar** – Sticky, blurred, with active state indicators.
- **Particle Background** – Floating gold sparks add depth and motion.
- **Loader Animation** – Dumbbell bounce while page loads.
- **3D Tilt Effects** – Training cards rotate with mouse movement.
